# R223W acceptance and hold conditions

## Acceptance criteria

R223W passes if it specifies:

1. teacher draft primary reading shell
2. canonical manuscript source references
3. no new teacher draft creation
4. collapsed / summary-first review ledger
5. v0.1 / v0.2 short difference summary
6. component trigger metadata-only view
7. safety banner and flags
8. decision outputs and hold conditions

## Allowed decision outputs

```text
PASS_CONTINUE_TO_R223X_REVIEW_ONLY_PILOT_GATE_FIXTURE
HOLD_FOR_R223W_GATE_SPEC_REDUCTION
HOLD_FORMAL_V0_2_NOT_READY
```

## Hold conditions

Hold if:

- R223W creates a new teacher draft
- R223W rewrites M/N/O teacher manuscript body
- review ledger is open as a field wall by default
- component trigger looks executable
- v0.2 is implied to be published
- any save/apply/writeback/runtime control appears
- formal R97B route is needed
